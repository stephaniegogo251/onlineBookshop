About Us


Welcome to Into the Unknown! Our humble little bookstore offers a wide variety of genres and authors to choose from. Here, every page holds a promise, an exciting adventure with every flip. From timeless classics to gripping new bestsellers, our store has it all! At Into the Unknown, we've got something for everyone. Our doors are open to the poets, the dreamers, the writers, the thinkers, the old, the young. Walk through our doors. Welcome home.


Our Mission:


At Into the Unknown, we believe in encouraging children to explore their imagination and enhance their reading. Our mission aims to inculcate an interest in reading, learning, and help children boost their creativity and originality. As part of our mission, we guarantee free access to all age appropriate books for children upto 15 years of age. Additionally, 10% of all our profits are donated to the Children's Education Fund to help provide an affordable education for all children in different parts of the world.
