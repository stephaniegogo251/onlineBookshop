Darling Girls

Sally Hepworth

★★★★☆ 4.20

Michael Mace, the security chief at a top secret lab known as the Beautification Research Project, is killed by a microbial infection that sweeps through the building — but then he mysteriously comes back to life. Michael is not the same after his experience: He now has the ability to interact with and control all manner of digital tools, from cell phones to mainframe computers. His newfound skills throw him into all kinds of pulse-pounding situations as he works to prevent a young teenager from joining a gang and tries to elude a brutal assassin who’s on his trail.
