Battle of Ink and Ice

Darell Hartman

★★★★☆ 3.85

At the turn of the 20th century, the New York Times and the New York Herald were staunch competitors, the former seeking to become the paper of record and the latter chasing circulation numbers. The competition’s stakes were raised in an unexpected way after the newspapers funded arctic expeditions, each seeking to be the first to reach the North Pole. But when both ventures claimed victory the ensuing public battle was filled with dubious evidence and vitriolic news coverage of two fascinating and thrilling adventures. Kirkus Reviews calls it “an engrossing and readable account.”

Genres:Suspense, Thriller, Action, Adventure, Adventure fiction, Science fiction
